What it does

Everything a restaurant needs

🪑

Table Management

Real-time table status — Available, Occupied, Reserved. Manager can toggle with one click.

📋

Reservations & Waitlist

Add walk-ins to the waitlist, assign them to tables, and notify them by email when ready.

🍽️

Customer Orders

Customers browse the menu, select dishes, and submit orders from the customer dashboard.

🔔

Table Watch Alerts

Customers can watch for a table — get an automatic email when their preferred table is free.

📧

Email Notifications

Gmail SMTP integration sends reminders, waitlist alerts, and order confirmations automatically.

📊

Manager Dashboard

Full overview of reservations, waitlist, orders, and dish stats — all in one place.
