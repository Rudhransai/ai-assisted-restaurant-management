Module 5 – Customer Feedback Aggregator
Assumptions:
- Kaggle/Seeded reviews
- Overall sentiment: DistilBERT
- Review categorization: facebook/bart-large-mnli
- Theme = Category
- Trend analysis from historical review, rating, sentiment and category data.
